Hi, I'm Jimmy

I've always been interested in the design and craft of all the things we make as a species, from being a child and wanting to understand how things work ‘under the hood’, to studying the alphabet and typography at university, I’ve always had a lot of curiosity surrounding how we can make our tools better, easier to use, more efficient and robust.

The driving force behind my curiosity is that I’m a determined problem solver, and a broad understanding of the world and its workings only helps me to make my solutions more varied and useful.

The most important tool we use collectively today is without doubt the internet. It's as important as the alphabet and printing-press as a way of communicating and sharing ideas. I’d like a better understanding of how it works, as well as how I can use and develop it further.

For the past few years I have worked in running bike shops or helping small cycle businesses grow, I am also chairman of a bicycle sporting association. My drive for efficiency and finding powerful solutions for problems has often brought me into contact with programming, whether it was excel spreadsheets, or online forms, I have had to self-study to make my systems better and find myself wanting for more.

Whilst in the cycle industry I have learnt a lot about organisation, communication and people management, but it’s time for me to close the chapter on working in this industry and to focus on riding for leisure and opening a new chapter of working life with the web. I hope that journey starts here.
